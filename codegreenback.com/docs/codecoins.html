CodeCoins

CodeCoins (CC) makes codegreenback more intresting to use. With the help of codecoins you can challenge other user in the community.

You can use codecoins to register in events and challenges(TurnYourTurn, Rushour, Face/Off) where you can win more codecoins and later you can redeem them into actual money directly to your bank account.

The Starting

CodeGreenBack offers 50 CC to new users with non-elite membership so that they can have a taste of this new ravishing platform for coders like you. The motive is simple “ worth your while ” the time you spent on coding should be worth it. So why not make it worth it while learning it.

Note

  • 1 CC (codecoins) has a value of 0.47INR while transferring
  • and 0.50 INR while buying
  • You can transfer your codecoins only when you have more than 100 CC and you are an Elite member of codegreenback
